Providence Healthtech has announced Joseph Down as Chief Accessibility Officer. Joseph is a disability advocate, accessibility advisor, and technology entrepreneur. A native-born Floridian, Joseph has called Winter Park home for the past twenty-two year. Joseph attended the University of Florida, where he received a degree in sport management. Following graduation, Joseph started his journey into advocacy by becoming a board member for Lighthouse Central Florida. Visually impaired since age seven, Joseph quickly realized serving people with disabilities was going to be his lifeâ€™s work. In the fall of 2019, Joseph founded Polaris Technology Group, a company dedicated to utilizing immersive technology to empower people with disabilities to have fulfilled, independent lives. In addition to Polaris, Joseph serves as an accessibility consultant for companies and a policy advocate for people with disabilities.

As Chief Accessibility Officer at Providence Healthtech, Joseph works to ensure our product offerings are built in an accessible-first way, allowing all users to benefit from healthcare technology software.
